Astro Launching

Astro’s Space Launch System will be the most powerful rocket we have ever built. When completed, SLS will enable astronauts to begin their journey to explore destinations far into the solar system
When Astro's SLS rocket takes off on its first flight, Artemis I, it will produce a maximum 8.8 million pounds of thrust, exerting more power than any rocket ever. Like many rockets, the rocket’s propulsion is delivered in stages
During a test of the SLS for Artemis I, engineers identified an issue with an RS-25 engine flight controller. Engineers will replace the engine controller while continuing integrated testing. Astro is reviewing launch opportunities for March and April.
